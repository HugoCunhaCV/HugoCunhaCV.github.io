cover letter

I am an IT professional with over twenty years of experience in information systems and technology infrastructure, now fully committed to the cybersecurity field. I hold the CompTIA Security+ (SY0-701) certification (June 2026), the ISC2 Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) (April 2026), and have recently completed the Google Cybersecurity Certificate and the Research Data Management and FAIR Principles course, reinforcing my technical foundations and my understanding of secure, structured information governance. I am applying for positions as a Cyber Security Analyst or Information Systems Security Analyst.

Throughout my career I have worked across enterprise network management, Linux and Windows systems administration, web server security, and the integration of collaborative platforms. This background gives me direct, operational experience with the infrastructure that security teams are tasked with protecting. I understand how systems are built, how they fail, and where threat actors find leverage.

My recent hands-on training has focused on Blue Team and SOC operations through the TryHackMe platform, where I have completed the SOC Level 1 Learning Path, covering practical modules in Splunk, Elastic Stack, MITRE ATT&CK, SOC L1 Alert Triage, SOC L1 Alert Reporting, and SOC Simulator. I am currently preparing for the Splunk Core Certified User certification to consolidate my SIEM skills. My grounding in data management and FAIR principles also positions me to contribute to information security governance, data classification, and policy enforcement within mature security programmes.

I am seeking a role where I can apply this combination of infrastructure depth and security specialisation — contributing to threat detection, incident response, and the continuous hardening of information systems. I am available for an interview at your convenience.

Kind regards,
Hugo Cunha
